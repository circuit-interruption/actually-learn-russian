Whether you consider the Russian language useful or not is irrelevant from our perspective. However, to us, it has become blatantly obvious that DuoLingo and a majority of 'LEARN RUSSIAN FAST!' content is a complete waste of time. Through trial and error, we have determined that the only true way to really learn a language is to immerse -- FULLY -- and this method will be the closest thing you can do to achieve that besides living abroad in Russia.

If you study the material on this website you will hopefully understand our joy at having a resource that is extremely accessible and allows you to see return almost immediately. You may even find your own ways of making this method even more useful. We do not, however, take responsibility for the introductory studying that is needed, such as learning how to read Cyrillic, Russian cases, forms, etc. There is an abundance of information on this that is for the most part uniform and just requires you to apply yourself and work towards a basic understanding of these subjects and constant practice.

We are so very thankful that we have been able to practice this learning method, and now we pass that opportunity on to you.


Essentials
You will need to install the following:
**Anki Flashcards** [ Create your own custom flashcards ]
**Language Reactor** [ Turn videos into interactive lessons ]
**Yomitan** [ A powerful pop-up dictionary ]

And this addon:
**Anki Connect** [ Enables external applications such as Yomitan to communicate with Anki ]
